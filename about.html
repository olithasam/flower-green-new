About Us

Why you should select us...

We are a well established Coco peat and coir manufacturing and exporting company in Sri Lanka with a strong clientele. Adapting and changing ourselves to meet the challenges of a changing environment, we have reached almost every nook and corner of the world. At present, we export our Coir products to our customers in the USA, UK, Italy, Canada, Egypt, U.A.E., Japan, South Africa, South Korea, Australia, New Zealand, Germany, and Russia. We make a difference in people's lives, everyday with our innovative, environmentally friendly products.

Most of our happy clients have selected us over many other Coco peat exporters because of few strong reasons; those reasons are the unmatchable five benefits that we offer to our clients.
