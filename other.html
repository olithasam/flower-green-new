Fresh Coconuts

Fresh Coconuts have a distinctive texture and a flavor and is grown on large acres yield where tropical sun is absorbed giving it a rich and a creamy taste.

We export fresh coconuts to any destination in the world in 20” or 40” dry/reefer containers. The products are packed in Coir Bags or Woven sacks to be exported by sea. The standarrd product would be; with husk, semi-husked, fully husked. Furthermore, the Coconuts are sized and husked according to customer requirements, which are approximately 12, 12-14 inches and above girth in size of 500 grams to 800 grams in weight and are available all year around.

Based on the required packing details, required girth or weight and maximum weight allowed in your port, we can offer you a very competitive price.

Fresh Coconut Oil

Coconut oil is extracted from the kernel or meat of matured coconut harvested from the coconut palm. Throughout the tropical world it has provided the primary source of fat in the diets of millions of people for generations. It has various applications in food, medicine, and industry. Coconut oil is very heat stable so it makes an excellent cooking and frying oil.

Packaging can be arranged according to the customer requirements.

Virgin Coconut Oil

Virgin Coconut Oil is a pure, natural, unrefined type of Coconut Oil. Clear and aromatic, with a mild natural flavor since it is produced directly from Fresh Coconut.

Extremely stable with an indefinite shelf life since it is almost saturated oil. It has very low amount of cholesterol compared to other edible oils and the chemical structure of medium chain fatty acids allows rapid digestion by the body and convert into energy.

An excellent source of energy. It is uniquely rich in Lauric fatty acid (48%), a prime ingredient in mother’s milk, which helps builds up an infant’s immune system.
